A game of life simulation. See wikipedia for details.

Instructions:

  • Hover over a square for information on it's current and future state.
  • Click on a square to flip it's state. This can be done when the game is running.
  • The start/stop buttons below the grid will commence and cease a game respectively.
  • Hover over the title to see this again.
